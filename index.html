Coding Starts Here

Who We Are

At Capybara Testing, we believe that everyone can learn to code when given a calm, supportive, and stress-free environment. Just like the gentle capybara, our community focuses on peaceful coexistence, social connection, and collective growth.

Our mission is to lower the barrier to entry for beginner programmers. We replace intimidating developer jargon and high-pressure assessments with friendly guidance, step-by-step sessions, and collaborative work. Whether you want to write your first line of HTML or build a simple web app, you are welcome in our herd.
