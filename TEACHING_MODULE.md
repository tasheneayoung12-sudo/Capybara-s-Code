# 🐾 Welcome to the Capybara Coding Club! 💻
## A Beginner-Friendly Guide to Exploring Your New Website 

Hello, Future Coding Champions! 🌟 

Meet **Capy**, your friendly, cozy capybara coding guide. Today, we are going to explore your brand-new website: **Capybara's Codes**! 

Below is a fun, easy-to-read teaching script and classroom guide you can use to show kids how to navigate, explore, and send a secret heart message on your website.

---

## 🗺️ Part 1: The Giant Map (Navigating the Page)

Think of our website like a beautiful, sunny river where a family of capybaras loves to hang out. To explore the river, we have special **stepping stones** at the very top of the page!

*   **🏡 Home:** This is the sunny riverbank where we start our adventure.
*   **🌱 About:** Meet the herd! This stone tells us why we are here (to learn coding together!).
*   **📚 Programs:** Choose your favorite coding playground.
*   **⭐ Student Work:** Read happy stories from other kid coders who learned here.
*   **✉️ Contact:** Send a letter to the capybaras!

---

## 🎒 Part 2: The Adventure Script (Teacher/Parent Read-Aloud)

*(Read this out loud to the class or your students as they look at the screen!)*

> **"Welcome, Adventurers! Today we are visiting Capybara's Codes. Let's look at the big screen together.**
>
> 🐾 **Step 1: The Big Greeting!** 🐾
> Look at the very top. See that cute capybara sitting next to a laptop? That's Capy! He is busy writing lines of code. What is coding? Coding is just like writing a list of friendly recipes or instructions for a computer so it knows how to make fun games and draw pictures!
>
> 🐾 **Step 2: Who are we?** 🐾
> Scroll your mouse down a little bit. See that happy capybara relaxing in the cool water with a pink flower on its head? That capybara is teaching us our most important rule: **Coding is calm and peaceful!** If your code has a bug (an error), we don't get frustrated. We just relax like a capybara and try again!
>
> 🐾 **Step 3: Choose Your Path!** 🐾
> Look at the three green cards. These are our programs:
> 1. **1-on-1 Sessions:** Getting help from a friendly teacher.
> 2. **Community Building:** Making great friends who also love computers.
> 3. **Special Projects:** Building cool real-world things together!
>
> 🐾 **Step 4: Voices from the Herd!** 🐾
> Read what Alex, Sarah, and Kevin have to say! They all started out just like you—not knowing anything about coding—and now they are super confident creators! Look at those golden stars!
> 
> 🐾 **Step 5: The Secret Heart Surprise!** 🐾
> Now for the most exciting part. Scroll down to the **Say Hello** box. Let's send a real-life message to the capybara herd! Let's type in our name, our email, and a nice note. Once you press that big **Send Message** button... keep your eyes wide open! A little capybara holding a giant red heart will pop up onto your screen to say **'Message Received!'**"

---

## 🏆 Part 3: The "Capybara Code Quest" Game

Make using the website an interactive game! Give your 3rd graders these three quests:

### 🔍 Quest 1: "Spot the Flowers"
*   **Goal:** Find the capybara relaxing with a pink flower on its head.
*   **Question to Ask:** *"What section of the website is this capybara guarding?"*
*   **Answer:** The **About** section!

### 🔍 Quest 2: "The Five-Star Starfish"
*   **Goal:** Find how many stars the students gave the Capybara Coding Club.
*   **Question to Ask:** *"Can you count the stars next to Sarah T.'s name?"*
*   **Answer:** 5 stars! ★★★★★

### 🔍 Quest 3: "The Heart Delivery"
*   **Goal:** Type a message, click Send, and make the secret heart capybara appear.
*   **Action:** Type a friendly greeting (like *"I love capybaras!"*) into the form and click **Send Message** to unlock the special heart-holding capybara popup!
